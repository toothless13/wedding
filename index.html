ABOUT THE VENUE

Milton Hill House is a hotel located about a 10 minute drive from Abingdon.

If you wish to stay the night at the hotel, please use the booking code CODE which should give you a 10% discount off your stay. You will need to call the Hotel on 01235 831474 to book using the code.

Check-in is from 2pm, but the hotel is happy to store any belongings behind the reception area.

There is ample parking at the venue you should want to drive and park your vehicle.

The closest station to the venue is Didcot Parkway, and from there it is about a 15 minute taxi journey.
